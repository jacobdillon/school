Combined Law
The combined gas law combines Charles's, Boyle's, and Gay-Lussac's gas laws into one formula.

The combined gas law combines Charles's, Boyle's, and Gay-Lussac's gas laws, which can only calculate volume and pressure, volume and temperature, or pressure and temperature, into a single equation. This formula enables you to calculate either pressure, volume, or temperature, with no constants. The equation for the combined gas law is P1V1⁄T1 = P2V2⁄T2.
